Home

This website is about hiking spots near Orem, Utah. I chose this topic because summer is approaching, and hiking is a great way to enjoy nature, explore the mountains, and experience different types of trails. The featured locations offer a variety of views and experiences, from steep climbs to waterfalls and suspension bridges.

Y Trail

A popular trail near Provo with a steep climb and a great view of Utah Valley.

Battle Creek Falls

A scenic trail with a waterfall, canyon views, and a peaceful outdoor setting.

Bear Canyon Suspension Bridge

A trail known for its suspension bridge and wide views of the valley.

CSS Techniques Used

CSS Grid

I used CSS Grid to organize the overall layout of the website, including the header, sidebar, main content, and footer.

Flexbox

I used Flexbox inside sections and content boxes to stack content vertically and control spacing.

Shadows and Rounded Corners

I added box shadows and rounded corners to make the site look cleaner and more modern.

Responsive Design

I added a media query so the layout adjusts on smaller screens and becomes easier to use on phones.

Why I picked this topic

I chose hiking because it is one of my favorite ways to spend my free time. Utah has many beautiful places to explore and capture amazing sunsets in a photo.
